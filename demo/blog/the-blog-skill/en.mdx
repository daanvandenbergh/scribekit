## The skill does the legwork

The bundled skill researches the topic, drafts on-brand copy, and fills the front-matter -
including a category from a small, reused taxonomy - so new posts slot straight into the grid.

## Review before you ship

A companion review step audits SEO, house style, and front-matter completeness before the post
goes live.
