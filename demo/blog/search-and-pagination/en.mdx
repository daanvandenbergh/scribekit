## Search is typo-tolerant

The search box runs a fuzzy match over each post's title, description, keywords, and
categories, so a near-miss still surfaces the right post.

## Scroll to load more

The grid reveals posts in batches and loads the next one when you reach the bottom, with a
"Load more" button as an explicit fallback.
